The version of ChemAxiomChemDomain.owl contained in this folder is an early attempt, last updated in 2009. It is made available here for legacy purposes, but its use is deprecated,
The ontology uses the Basic Formal Ontology (BFO) as an upper ontology and is licenced under a GPL v.3 licence.
Current releases of the ontology use the General Formal Ontology (GFO) and the Scimantica Science Ontology instead and have been re-licenced under a Creative Commons Attribution licence.
